#daretoimagine

WHEN WE UNLEASH OUR IMAGINATION AND TURN IT INTO ACTION, WE CAN MOVE THE WORLD.

#DARETOIMAGINE:
A U.S. DEPARTMENT OF ARTS AND CULTURE NATIONAL ACTION

From October 10th-18th, people across the United States are practicing radical imagination and creating the world they want to see. What if instead of another holiday commemorating the past, we took time to envision and celebrate the future?

Join us and explore what’s possible. Post your own visions of the Future using #DareToImagine on Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram and find an Imagination Station near you.

You are the frontlines of change

This is a concept we think about a lot at CTZN. How can we empower the individual to show the good they do in the world and connect with causes on a deeper level.

We think you’re awesome. And we truly believe in the power of the individual to push movements forward.

You might know that our sister company is GOOD Magazine. We’ve just kicked off the Be a CTZN series where we gather intimate reflections on citizenship from movement leaders.

The first post is from Adam Horowitz, founder of the U.S. Department of Arts and Culture. There’s a preview below, and you can read the full article here. We’ll have a new post every week, including contributions from master storyteller MK Asante, Motherstruck star Staceyann Chinn, gangster gardener Ron Finley, billionaire philanthroper Manoj Bhargava, and park builder Gia Biagi, among others.

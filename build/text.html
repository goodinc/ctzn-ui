Curator Tips

While CTZN’s true success comes in helping to foster a collaborative community where individuals can connect and share in a collective Push, group administrators, or Curators, have a few bonus powers that will help them maintain structure.

Here is an overview of what Curators can do.

Invite People via Email

As a Curator, you can invite your community into your Push with a press of a button. Simply press “Invite More People via Email” and CTZN will automatically connect to your email client (works extra well on mobile). Add in as many email addresses as you’d like and press send. CTZN will send everyone an invite email that contains a direct link into your Push.

Promote People to Curators

Share the power of curator with your organization’s most trusted members–or take it away, instantly and without the support of a CTZN team member.

Hide Posts

While we encourage everyone to share a wide range of content on CTZN ranging from “This is how I want to change LA” to “Look at our office’s pet chickens!”, we understand that not all content fits our Push’s purpose. As Curator, you have the option of Hiding posts from the feed by pressing on the “...” button at the bottom of a post and pressing “Hide”. This will make the post invisible to everyone but the original poster and those users who have already interacted with the post. This will also prevent the post from appearing in the Morning Digest.

Curate the Live Channel

A Curator, however, is ultimately responsible for Curating the Push’s best content. Next to the “Love” and “Comment” buttons, Curator’s have an additional Star Button which allows them to Feature posts. When a post is Featured, it becomes publicly viewable on your Push’s public site. From this public site, featured content can be made into a Live Slideshow that can be updated automatically and broadcast on your website (through a widget) and on monitors and projectors.
